At the Company’s sole discretion, the Company may choose to substitute a similar loyalty program for any of the Legacy Programs or for the Loyalty Program at any time immediately upon notice to active Loyalty Members. If any of the Legacy Programs or the Loyalty Program are terminated, all unredeemed Points will be forfeited without any obligation or liability, and no Redemption Award claims or promotional Award claims will be honored after the conclusion of the notice period.
