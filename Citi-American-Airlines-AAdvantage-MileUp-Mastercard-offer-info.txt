How many miles do you earn when you purchase flights with the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard®?
AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® cardmembers earn two miles for every dollar spent on eligible American Airlines purchases - and every mile earned gets you one step closer to redeeming exciting rewards. AAdvantage® miles can be redeemed for flights to nearly 1,000 destinations worldwide, flight upgrades, car rentals and hotels.
How can I apply for the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard®?
We aim to make the application process for the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® as easy and straightforward as possible. Learn more and apply online. 
Does the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® offer rewards?
Absolutely! The AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® issued by Citi® was designed for cardmembers to earn miles simply based on their everyday purchases. These rewards allow cardmembers to:Earn two miles for every dollar spent at grocery stores and on eligible American Airlines purchasesEarn one mile for every dollar spent on all other purchases
What rewards does the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® offer?
The AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® offers cardmembers many benefits, which include:You can earn two miles for every dollar spent on eligible American Airlines purchases and at grocery storesPlus earn one mile for every dollar spent on all of your other purchasesEnjoy a 25% savings on in-flight food and beverage purchases when you use your card on American Airlines flights
Does the AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® have an annual fee?
One of the premium perks of being an AAdvantage MileUp℠ Mastercard® cardmember is that there is no annual fee.1
