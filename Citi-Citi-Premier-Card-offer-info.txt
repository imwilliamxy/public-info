What are the benefits of the Citi Premier℠ Card?

The Citi Premier℠ Card offers several benefits, including the ability to enjoy rewards through the ThankYou® Rewards program:Earn ThankYou® Points per $1 spent on your Citi Premier℠ Card.Earn 3X on Travel, including GasEarn 2X on Restaurants and EntertainmentEarn 1X on all other purchasesAdditional benefits of this popular travel credit card include no foreign transaction fees on purchases1, as well as easy points transfer to certain airline loyalty programs, including TrueBlue®, Virgin Atlantic Flying Club and Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer.



How many points do I earn for my purchases with the Citi Premier℠ Card?

You will earn 3 ThankYou® Points per $1 spent on the purchases at the following places: AirlinesHotelsCruisesCar rental agenciesTravel agenciesGas stationsRailwaysCommuter transportationFerriesSubwaysTaxisBridge and road tollsParking lotsBus linesAnd moreYou will earn 2 ThankYou® Points per $1 spent on purchases at:RestaurantsCafésBarsLoungesLive Entertainment such as concerts, sporting events and moreMovie TheatersAmusement ParksYou will earn 1 ThankYou® Point per $1 spent on all other purchases, including:GroceriesPurchases at drug storesUtility paymentsBill paymentsPurchases at retailersAnd more



Are there foreign transaction fees1 for the Citi Premier℠ Card?

There are no foreign transaction fees1 with the Citi Premier℠ Card, whether you're purchasing tickets for a museum in London, buying dinner in Milan or paying for a hotel in Tokyo.
