Citi® Double Cash Card
        Earn cash back rewards on every purchase with the Citi® Double Cash card. You get unlimited 1% cash back when you buy, plus another 1% back as you pay for those purchases. Make your money go further with one of Citi's best cash back credit cards.

Rewards
Earn Cash Back TWICE
This card rewards you in two ways, so now you don't have to just spend more to earn more. First, you earn 1% unlimited cash back on every purchase you make.
Then, as you pay for those purchases – whether it's all at once or over time – you earn an additional 1% cash back on those credit card payments.
To earn cash back, pay at least the minimum due on time.
No Caps and No Category Restrictions
                                Unlike many other cash back credit cards, Citi Double Cash has no restricting categories to keep track of or enroll in.2
                                There are also no caps on the total amount of cash back you earn.
                                
Cash Back Program Details
What Earns Cash Back
                           When you make a purchase, you earn 1% unlimited cash back. Then, you earn an additional 1% cash back as you make payments for that purchase, whether you pay in full or over time. To earn cash back, pay at least the minimum due on time. You earn cash back on all your purchases and on payments based on your purchases. You do not earn cash back based on balance transfer, cash advances, account fees, interest or returned items.
Redeem
                            Choose to receive your cash back as a statement credit, direct deposit, or check; or convert to points.

Does the Citi® Double Cash Card have a foreign transaction fee?
Yes, the Citi® Double Cash Card does carry a foreign transaction fee, which is 3% of the U.S. dollar amount of each purchase made in a foreign currency. 1
What kind of card is Citi® Double Cash?
The Citi® Double Cash Card is a Mastercard credit card that provides Cardmembers with cash back rewards. Cardmembers earn 2% cash back by earning 1% cash back on purchases, plus an additional 1% cash back as they pay for those purchases. To earn cash back, pay at least the minimum payment on time. And, with no annual fee 1, the Citi® Double Cash Card offers another great reason for Cardmembers to use it for their everyday purchases.
Do Citi® Double Cash rewards expire?
Cardmembers' Double Cash rewards only expire when Cardmembers have not earned any cash back from purchases or payments for those purchases within a 12-month period. In the event that the account is closed or converted, the Cardmember will no longer earn cash rewards and the accumulated cash rewards will be forfeited. Additionally, you will no longer be able to reinstate purchases if your Card Account is closed or converted. Cardmembers are advised to redeem any eligible cash rewards prior to account closure. 
How do you redeem Citi® Double Cash rewards?
You can choose to redeem your Citi® Double Cash Card cash rewards as follows:Request a check for at least $25 up to the total cash rewards balance at the time you redeem;Redeem for a statement credit to your Card Account for at least $25 up to the total cash rewards balance at the time you redeem;Redeem for a credit to your linked Citi savings or checking account or to a checking account from which you have paid a Citi Credit Card bill at least two timesConvert your cash rewards to ThankYou Points
