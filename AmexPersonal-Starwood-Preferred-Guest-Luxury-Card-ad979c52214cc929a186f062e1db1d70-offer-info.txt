BAGGAGE INSURANCE PLAN♦︎
Travel more comfortably knowing you can be covered by the Baggage Insurance Plan♦ for eligible lost, damaged, or stolen baggage when you purchase the entire fare for a Common Carrier ticket (e.g. plane, train, ship, or bus) on an eligible Card. Please read important exclusions and restrictions.
